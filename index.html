I am a political scientist with a background in statistics pursuing a PhD at ETH Zurich in Switzerland. In my research, I study how coalition governments make decisions in the European Union and how conflict within coalitions affects the EU decision-making process more generally.

In addition, I contribute to Frank Schimmelfennig's ERC-funded project EUROBORD for which we study the EU and the (dis)integration process from a bordering perspective.
